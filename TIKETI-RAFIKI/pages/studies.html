Bsc.IT and Tiketi Rafiki SJUT family students pay a visit to the Geological Survey of Tanzania(GST) in Dodoma for practical Training.

BSc.IT and Tiketi Rafiki SJUT members shine in Tiketi Rafiki Christmas Challenge, Develop Innovative System.

The Tiketi Rafiki hosted a Christmas Challenge for BSc.IT students called the Tiketi Rafiki Tech Christmas Challenge. The challenge involved students developing various systems, and awards were presented to the winners. Our students successfully completed the challenges such as Online Employee Attendance System and Simple Inventory Control System for Small Businesses.
