Join with us and be part of the success

Tiketi Rafiki SJUT family is a student-led association comprising of IT students at St. John's University of Tanzania under the mentorship and guidance of Tiketi Rafiki. The purpose is to provide an opportunity for students to benefits from Tiketi Rafiki products and services, finding ways to support their passion and dreams using technology, and link them with other industry partners.
It was launched on 10 Dec 2022 at SJUT Dodoma by Dr. Amsabi Mrimi, a member of parliament for Serengeti. This family has been embraced by BSc.IT students, both internal and external staff, as well as individuals from various sectors. We welcome everyone to join our family.

Our Mission

The Tiketi Rafiki SJUT Family is dedicated to building a supportive and inclusive environment for all SJUT members. Our mission is to empower students by fostering connections, shairing experiences and providing resources and mentorship networks. This collaborative approach aims to propel students towards academic success.

Our Vision

The Tiketi Rafiki SJUT Family envisions itself as a thriving community that fosters both personal and professional growth for its members. We aim to be a central hub for student success, offering valuable resources and exemplifying the power of collaboration within the SJUT community.
