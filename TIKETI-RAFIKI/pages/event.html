Tiketi Rafiki SJUT family celebrated with Tumain Foundation.

The Tiketi Rafiki SJUT Family partnered with Tumaini Foundation to host a joyful Christmas event for the children in December 2022. Highlighted by guest of honor, Miss Tanzania 2022 Halima Kopwe, the event brought together SJUT students, Tumaini children and festive cheer. It showcased the power of collaboration and social responsibility, leaving everyone with lasting memories.
